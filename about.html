About POA&More

POA&More is a dedicated Dubai-based service company specializing in preparing and facilitating services.

We provide a comprehensive suite of document processing solutions, including drafting, notarization support, true copy attestation, legalization, and company establishment services.

We bring two decades of experience in the UAE and GCC markets to deliver exceptional legal services.

Our team

comprises seasoned service consultants and skilled translators who understand the intricacies of the UAE’s landscape.

We are committed to providing tailored solutions that simplify your transactions while ensuring accuracy and efficiency.

With a focus on customer satisfaction, POA&More is your reliable partner for your service facilitation.

Mission

Our mission is to simplify transactions for individuals and businesses by delivering efficient, reliable, and personalized services that save time and ensure accuracy.

To be the leading provider of services in the UAE, recognized for our commitment to customer satisfaction and transparency.
