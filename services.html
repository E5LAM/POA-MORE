Notarization & Attestation

E-Notarization

Coordinating and finalizing all kinds of notarial services with Notary Public online. E-Notarization (or Electronic Notarization) is the process of performing notarization digitally, where the notary and the document signer interact through an online platform rather than in person. This service provides flexibility and convenience, especially for individuals or businesses needing notarization without the need to meet a notary in person.

Apostille Attestation Services

The purpose of the Apostille is to eliminate the need for lengthy legalization processes when authenticating documents for international use between countries that are part of the Hague Apostille Convention.

Verification

A certified true copy is a lawyer-issued document with a signature and stamp confirming it's an exact copy of the original. This attestation is often needed for passports, DEWA/utility bills, and employment letters by government authorities, embassies, banks, and other institutions worldwide.
